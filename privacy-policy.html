Overview

The short version

OrgoFlash does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

OrgoFlash is an organic chemistry flashcard app designed to help students study reaction mechanisms. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

01

Information we collect

OrgoFlash does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal details to use it.

The only data the app stores is your study progress â€” which decks you have attempted, your answer history, and accuracy statistics. This data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) and never leaves your device.

02

How your data is used

Your locally stored study data is used solely to display your progress and accuracy statistics within the app. It is not used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.

You can delete all stored data at any time by tapping Reset All Data in the Data tab of the app, or by uninstalling OrgoFlash from your device.

03

Third-party services

OrgoFlash does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy, which is separate from this policy and outside our control.

04

Children's privacy

OrgoFlash is designed for college-level students and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected by the app at all, there is no special risk to younger users.
